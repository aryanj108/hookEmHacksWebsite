About Hook 'em Hacks

Hook 'em Hacks is the University of Texas at Austin's premier student-run hackathon, bringing together innovative minds from across the country to build, create, and compete over an exciting 24-hour period.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, Hook 'em Hacks provides the perfect environment to learn new skills, meet amazing people, and bring your ideas to life. Join us for workshops, mentorship, free meals, and tons of swag!

This year's theme celebrates innovation and collaboration, with challenges spanning web development, mobile apps, AI/ML, hardware, and more. Compete for amazing prizes while making lifelong connections with fellow Longhorns and students from around the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend?

▼

Hook 'em Hacks is open to all current college students, regardless of major or experience level. High school students are welcome with parental consent.

How much does it cost?

▼

Hook 'em Hacks is completely FREE! We'll provide meals, snacks, swag, and everything you need for an amazing weekend of hacking.

What should I bring?

▼

Bring your laptop, chargers, valid student ID, and enthusiasm! We recommend bringing a sleeping bag if you plan to rest during the event.

Do I need a team?

▼

Teams can have up to 4 people. You can come with a team or form one at the event during our team formation session. Solo hackers are also welcome!

What can I build?

▼

Anything! Web apps, mobile apps, hardware projects, games, AI/ML projects—if you can code it, you can build it. We'll have various tracks and prizes.
